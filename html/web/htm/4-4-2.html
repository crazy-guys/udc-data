Learn the basics of blockchain with UDC 2019 Free Lecture

While UDC 2019 is a celebration of expert developers,
we also want to welcome blockchain newcomers.

For those interested in the basics of blockchain, UDC provides the Blockchain Free Lecture
to provide a better understanding of the technology and ecosystem.

Even if you are not currently involved in the industry,
UDC 2019 Blockchain Free Lecture is a great opportunity
to learn the basics of blockchain and satisfy your curiosities.

OVERVIEW

  • Date & TimeSession 1 : Aug. 6 (Tue) 14:00~18:00
    Session 2 : Aug. 13 (Tue) 14:00~18:00
    Session 3 : Aug. 20 (Tue) 14:00~18:00
  • VenueDunamu HQ (Gangnam, Seoul)
  • Curriculum- Overview and History of Blockchain, Cryptocurrencies, and Cryptography
  • ParticipantsNovices interested in blockchain technology
  • Official Language Korean (Korean-English simultaneous interpretation will NOT be provided.)
